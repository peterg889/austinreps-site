About Us

Real estate built on trust, experience, and results.

Austin Real Estate Partners is an independent boutique brokerage led by Bob McKenna, serving the Austin area since 2003.

Bob McKenna, MBA

Bob McKenna is a licensed Real Estate Broker and Property Tax Consultant with more than 20 years of experience in residential real estate. As Broker and Owner of Austin Real Estate Partners, he has personally overseen more than 1,000 successful real estate transactions, including 500+ in which he directly represented sellers, buyers, and investors.

Before entering real estate, Bob spent over 15 years in Corporate Finance, Corporate Strategy, Product Marketing, and Business Development within the technology sector. He holds an MBA from Rice University. This combination of business acumen and deep real estate expertise allows him to deliver clear-headed, strategic guidance for every client.

The Brokerage

Austin Real Estate Partners is a recipient of the Better Business Bureau's Torch Award for Marketplace Ethics and has been recognized by the Austin Business Journal as a Top 25 Small Team for production. We focus exclusively on residential real estate across Austin and the surrounding communities, serving buyers, sellers, investors, landlords, and tenants.

Our approach is straightforward: honest advice, responsive communication, and a commitment to acting in our clients' best interests at every stage of the transaction.

Associations & Service

Bob is a member of the National Association of REALTORS® (NAR), Texas REALTORS® (TXR), the Austin Board of REALTORS® (ABOR), the San Antonio Board of REALTORS® (SABOR), the Houston Association of REALTORS® (HAR), North Texas Real Estate Information Systems (NTRIES), and the Central Texas MLS (CTXMLS). He served on the Austin Board of REALTORS® Board of Directors from 2017–2019.

Property Tax Consulting

In addition to brokerage services, Bob is a certified Property Tax Consultant, helping property owners navigate the tax protest process. This service is offered through his property tax consulting practice, PXN.
